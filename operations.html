Operations

The South African Secret Service is dedicated to ensuring the security and stability of the nation. Through a combination of intelligence gathering, counterintelligence efforts, and international collaboration, we work to safeguard our citizens and uphold our sovereignty.

Our operations encompass a wide range of activities, including:

  • Threat assessment and analysis
  • Counterterrorism efforts
  • Counterintelligence operations
  • Cooperation with international intelligence agencies
  • Cybersecurity initiatives

While specific details of our operations remain confidential for security reasons, our commitment to national security and the well-being of South Africa is unwavering.
