Overview

Welcome to the Cybersecurity Division of the South African Secret Service. Our mission is to protect the nation's digital infrastructure and sensitive information from cyber threats.

We work tirelessly to ensure the security and privacy of our citizens, government systems, and critical assets.

Our Responsibilities

Our responsibilities include:

  • Monitoring and detecting cyber threats
  • Conducting risk assessments and vulnerability analyses
  • Developing and implementing cybersecurity strategies
  • Collaborating with international partners on cyber intelligence
