About Us

The South African Secret Service (SASS) is a dedicated intelligence agency committed to safeguarding the security, stability, and sovereignty of our nation. With a rich history and a relentless pursuit of excellence, SASS plays a pivotal role in identifying and countering threats to our country's interests.

Established to serve the people of South Africa, SASS operates under the oversight of the Ministry of State Security. Our agency's focus spans from countering terrorism and espionage to addressing cyber threats and contributing to international intelligence cooperation.

Our Mission

At SASS, our mission is clear: to gather accurate, actionable intelligence that empowers our leaders and decision-makers to make informed choices in the best interest of our nation. We are committed to promoting stability, protecting critical infrastructure, and ensuring the safety of our citizens and allies.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations.
  • Professionalism: We maintain a team of dedicated experts who excel in their fields.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with other security agencies to address complex challenges.
  • Adaptability: We evolve to counter emerging threats in an ever-changing world.

Leadership

Under the guidance of Director Mr Robert McBride, SASS is led by a team of seasoned professionals who bring years of experience in intelligence, security, and diplomacy.

Organizational Structure

SASS operates under a well-defined organizational structure designed to maximize efficiency and effectiveness. Our various divisions and units work collaboratively to fulfill our mandate while adhering to legal and ethical considerations.
