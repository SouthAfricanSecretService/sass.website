South African Secret Service Mandate

The South African Secret Service (SASS) is entrusted with a critical mandate aimed at safeguarding the nation's security and well-being. Our primary responsibilities include:

  1. Gathering and analyzing intelligence to assess threats to national security, sovereignty, and stability.
  2. Conducting counterintelligence operations to detect and neutralize espionage and subversive activities within our borders.
  3. Collaborating with other national security agencies to share intelligence and coordinate efforts to counter emerging threats.
  4. Contributing to the protection of critical infrastructure, government officials, and citizens.
  5. Engaging in international intelligence cooperation to promote global security and address transnational challenges.
  6. Addressing cyber threats by securing digital assets, preventing cyberattacks, and fostering cybersecurity awareness.
  7. Upholding the highest ethical and legal standards in all our operations and interactions.

By fulfilling these responsibilities, SASS plays a vital role in ensuring a secure, stable, and prosperous South Africa for all its citizens.
