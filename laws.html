Introduction

This document outlines the laws governing the operations and activities of the South African Secret Service (SASS). It is a work in progress and subject to revisions.

Purpose and Mandate

The purpose of the SASS is to safeguard South Africa's national security by collecting, analyzing, and disseminating intelligence to support informed decision-making and protect the country's interests.

The mandate of the SASS includes:

  • Gathering intelligence on threats to national security.
  • Conducting counterintelligence operations.
  • Contributing to cybersecurity efforts.
  • Supporting foreign policy objectives through intelligence.

Operations and Functions

Details about the specific operations and functions of the SASS will be provided in subsequent revisions of this document.

Accountability and Oversight

The SASS operates under the oversight of the Ministry of State Security and is subject to legal and ethical standards that ensure transparency, professionalism, and adherence to the law.

Amendments and Revisions

This document is a work in progress and will undergo revisions to accurately reflect the legal framework governing the South African Secret Service.
