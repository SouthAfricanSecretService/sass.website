South African Secret Service Glossary

South African Secret Service (SASS)
The national intelligence agency responsible for gathering intelligence and supporting national security objectives.
Counterintelligence
Activities aimed at detecting and countering espionage and intelligence threats.
Threat Assessment
The evaluation of potential risks and threats to national security.
Intelligence Gathering
The process of collecting information to analyze and inform decision-making.
Cybersecurity
Efforts to protect digital infrastructure and data from cyber threats.
National Security
The protection of a nation's sovereignty, citizens, and critical assets.
